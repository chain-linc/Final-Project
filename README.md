## How to play
how you will be playing the game is dragging and dropping sheeps onto placable tiles (the outline of a sheep)

1. So first when you log on you will be welecomed to a welcome menu
2. Then when you click okay you will see 15 different levels to shoose from
3. then you can click on one and it will load you into the game
4. when you are in the game you will have the
   1.  amount of sheep needed to be placed
   2.  amount of regular sheep avalible
   3.  amount of mountain sheep avalible (the ones with the horns so like a mountain goat)
   4.  amount of life jacket sheep (the ones with the yellow canistors with the red cross on them)
5. so you will have to drag the correct number of sheep and make the correct fromation of sheep to make it though the maze
   1. sheep canot move though mountains, fences, or water (with exceptions)
   1. regular sheep do nothing
   2. mountain sheep can go though mountains
   3. life jacket sheep can go though water
6. you will be able to move the sheep all in unision forward, left, right, or back but none of the sheep can go though tiles their kind is not designed for
7. if a sheep hits a fence/montain/water the sheep will try going into then a visual "explosion" will happen then all the sheep will move back to their original spots (this will all take about 0.5s)
8. after you navigate though the maze only one of your sheep have to reach the goal (a staff) and you win the level!
